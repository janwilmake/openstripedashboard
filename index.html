How It Works

Get started in seconds with your existing Stripe account

1

Enter Your Stripe Secret Key

Provide your Stripe secret key to securely access your payment link data. The key is stored locally in your browser.

2

Automatic Data Fetching

We automatically fetch all your active payment links and their associated transactions from the Stripe API.

3

Analyze Your Revenue

View comprehensive analytics, drill down into transactions, and track your payment link performance over time.

Your Data Stays Private

We take security seriously. Your Stripe secret key is stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to our servers. All API calls are made directly from your browser to Stripe.

🔒 Local Storage
🛡️ No Server Storage
🔐 Direct API Calls

For maximum security, you can host this dashboard yourself or deploy it to your own infrastructure.
